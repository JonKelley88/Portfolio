About Me

I am a digital artist who learned how to write code. My passion for computers and computer graphics started when a friend gave me a copy of Photoshop 7.0 in 2003. Since then, my fascination has driven me to explore everything from graphic design to 3D modeling to game development.

A whole new world opened up for me once I dove into programming. The possibilities that arise from the ability to create functional and interactive content from scratch was just too exciting for me to ignore. I realized that Web Development would be the perfect field for me as it is a challenging but fun blend of creativity and logic.
